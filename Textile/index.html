About Our Store

Our Story Since 2023

Mahendhra Textiles and Family Shopping has been serving the Pattukkottai community for over 2 years. Established in 2023, we take pride in offering the finest quality textiles, clothing, and household essentials at competitive prices.

Our spacious showroom at GG Tower, Chinniah Street, Mainckam Colony houses one of the largest collections in the region, with over 10,000 products in stock at any given time. We specialize in:

  • Traditional silk and cotton sarees
  • Men's formal and casual wear
  • Children's clothing and school uniforms
  • Home textiles and furnishings
  • Wedding and festival collections

Our team of 15 experienced staff members is dedicated to helping you find exactly what you need with personalized service and expert advice.
